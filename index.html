MACROMAXXING

Log what you eat, watch the ring fill — and when you're stuck at 3pm with half your macros left, hit Solve and it does the arithmetic: real portions, real meals, landing you on your numbers.

Tip: long-press any food card to drag it into the order you actually eat.

THREE BUTTONS, ONE BRAIN

Every food carries three switches. Solve reads them.

Like — favored in every Solve. If it can make a decent plate, it gets a seat.
Dislike — never suggested again, in any mode. Thumb it down right inside a Solve result and it re-solves around it.
Have at home — tells Solve a food from the big database is in your kitchen right now, no shopping mode needed.

Find them on food cards, in the gram sheet, and next to every Solve suggestion.
